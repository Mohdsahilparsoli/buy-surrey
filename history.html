Our History

Our history is the history of the Surrey bike in America

The history of two (2) person, four (4) person and six (6) person Surrey bikes in America is the history of the INTERNATIONAL SURREY COMPANY LTD.,THE Surrey Company. Our rich and proud heritage is unmatched in the United States. We did not invent the Surrey bike, but we did invent the Surrey rental business

In 1970, Cliff Marshall Imports in Dennisport, Massachusetts introduced for sale the Selene Surrey bike, an Italian-made Surrey bike produced by Mecart. Later that same year, The Surrey Company was founded. The Surrey Company opened as Beach Bike Rental and operated rental locations in Kissimmee, Florida and Galveston, Texas with great success, renting cycles of all types, including the Selene, the Rosalie (the Gitane Rosalie, a uni-frame four-wheel cycle manufactured in France) and the PPV (People Powered Vehicle similar to our new Impello™).

… As a Rental Operator

The Surrey Company has rented 2 person bikes and other cycles for over 40 years. Our rental experience is absolutely unparalleled in the United States. As a rental operator, The Surrey Company gained first-hand knowledge of the abusive nature of the rental industry and the shortened useful life of Surrey bikes due to such abuse. Over 50 years ago, The Surrey Company began to evaluate the weaknesses of the other products on the market and worked with the importer to communicate these weaknesses to the manufacturer.

he Surrey Company uses our extensive knowledge and experience to constantly innovate and improve our products by merging yesterday’s lessons with tomorrow’s bicycle technology. From battery powered LED headlamps, aluminum alloy rims and stainless steel spokes to our Easy 7 Speed Shimano Gearing Package and the electric pedal assist Hitch Hiker, The Surrey Company innovates while the others imitate.

As the Industry Leader!

From families to mom and pop shops to large institutional buyers, The Surrey Company supplies them all, which is why we are recognized as the industry leader.
